Stream your Spotify into any Discord voice channel.

A self-hosted desktop app that streams your own Spotify audio straight into a Discord voice channel. It runs on your PC, and nothing routes through a third-party server.

Download for Windows How it works
Windows 10/11 Spotify Premium Open source
01

Self-hosted

The bot runs from your PC. Your Spotify account and the audio never reach a third-party server.

02

Real Spotify audio

It streams exactly what plays on your desktop, including your own queue and library.

03

Plain-language commands

A bundled local model parses requests, so commands don't depend on exact syntax.

04

Open source

The full source is on GitHub. Installers are published from it on every tagged release.

Setup, start to finish.

01

Install greenroom

Download and run the Windows installer. A guided setup handles the rest.

02

Connect Discord & Spotify

Paste your bot token and Spotify keys. Built-in video guides walk through each portal.

03

Invite the bot & link

Invite the bot to your server, then run /login to link Spotify once.

04

Run /play

Join a voice channel and run /play with a song, playlist, or Spotify link.
