How it works

From your Spotify to their ears in four steps.

greenroom captures Spotify from your PC through a virtual audio cable and streams it straight into the voice channel your bot joins. Everything runs locally — your account, your machine, your control.

1

Install greenroom

Download and run the Windows installer. A guided setup walks you through everything else.

2

Connect Discord & Spotify

Paste your bot token and Spotify keys. Built-in video guides show each click.

3

Invite the bot & link Spotify

Run /login in Discord to link your Spotify account once.

4

Play

Run /play with a song, playlist, or Spotify link. The channel hears it live.
